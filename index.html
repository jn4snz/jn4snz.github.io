Research Interests

I am interested in the fluid dynamics and physics of fluid flows that affect the oceans and climate. To this end, I am investigating the energetics and dynamics of convection and the overturning circulation in the oceans.

I use a combination of theory, model development, and analysis of data from idealized and realistic model simulations and from observations.
